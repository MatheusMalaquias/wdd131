Website Planning Document

Site Name

Site name: My resume

I chose this name because the idea is to share my experiences and professional background.

Optional domain: myresume.dev

Site Purpose

The site presents my professional background, skills, work experience, and contact information for recruiters and potential employers.

Scenarios

  • What are this person's professional skills?
  • How can I get in touch regarding a job opportunity?

Color Schema

  • Dark green (#2E7D32): headings and highlights
  • Light beige (#F1F8E9): page background
  • Dark gray (#333333): main text

Typography

  • Montserrat: usada nos títulos e cabeçalhos para dar um visual moderno e profissional.
  • Open Sans: usada no texto do corpo para facilitar a leitura.
